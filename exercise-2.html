Who is Yuma?

Born in Osaka, Yuma Sumi is a former professional ballet dancer at State Ballet of Georgia in Tbilisi, Georgia.

* * *

As a ballet dancer, I trained in Japan, Portugal and Netherlands, and performed internationally such as at Bolshoi theater in Moscow. My journey as a freelancer in Japan began this March, as I gave my last performance with SBG in Israel. On graduating from Code Chrysalis Foundations Course this summer, I moved to Itoshima, Fukuoka, to learn sustainable living skills and identify local and social issues.

What brought him to CC7?

Yuma is focusing on what he would call the collaboration of art and business .

* * *

Back in my days in Tbilisi, I had a knee injury that forced me to stop dancing for a while, and to come back to Japan all the way just to see good doctors, who actually could not give me any useful advices. It was simply annoying that I could not find anyone who could possibily help me out, until I bump into the one - only possbily. That was the first time the idea to become a software engineer to support those suffering, without getting bothered with restriction by location, came into my mind.

Fun facts about Yuma

Yuma is a writer at Lifehacker Japan - at the same time, he grows rice.

* * *

"Itoshima Share House" , where I currently live, has wonderful residents such as writer, cook, hunter and carpenter. With the unique concept of this house, "sustainable lifestyle of food, job and energy," all of them grow rice together so they do not have to buy one for a year at all (which is just one of the good examples of their attempts.)
